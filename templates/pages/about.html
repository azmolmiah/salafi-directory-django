Salafi directory has been created to help direct people to the salafi only sources. This has come about to help those new and old to know who and where we can take Islamic knowledge from.

  1. Listed classes are scheduled regularly, on a particular day of the week.
  2. Listed lectures are scheduled with specific dates.
  3. Listed teachers listed with country of residence.
  4. Listings according to time zones.
  5. Contact organisations if contact form displayed.
  6. Filter through organisations, lectures, classes and teachers.
  7. Listed organisations are set up for you.
