About us

A Salafi only directory website, to know who and where to take knowledge from.

  1. Home

بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم

This directory website has started because of those who are new to the Salafi Dawah and wish to know about who take Islamic knowledge from, where to take knowledge from and how they can receive any assistance in their journey to Salafiya.

Salafi Directory aims to keep you, your family and friends protected from platforms which mislead the people away from the true teachings of Islam.

If you're ever anywhere new, and want to know the nearest source of salafiya, use our ineteractive maps feature.

Features
  1. Find listed Salafi classes that are scheduled on a weekly basis.
  2. Find listed Salafi lectures are scheduled with specific dates and ranges of dates.
  3. Find listed Salafi teachers with the country of residence.
  4. Salafi Listings according to time zones.
  5. Filter through Salafi organisations, lectures, classes and teachers.
  6. Listed Salafi organisations are set up for you.
  7. Find a Salafi organisation anywhere in the world

Direct your family and friends to salafi only sources.
